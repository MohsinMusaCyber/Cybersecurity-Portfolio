# Network Devices

## Router

A router connects different networks together and forwards data packets between them.

Typical use:
- Connects a home or office network to the Internet.

---

## Switch

A switch connects devices within the same Local Area Network (LAN).

Typical use:
- Connecting computers, printers and servers.

---

## Firewall

A firewall monitors and filters incoming and outgoing network traffic according to security rules.

Typical use:
- Blocking malicious traffic.
- Allowing authorised communications.

---

## Wireless Access Point (WAP)

A Wireless Access Point provides Wi-Fi connectivity to wireless devices.

Typical use:
- Homes
- Offices
- Public Wi-Fi

---

## Modem

A modem converts signals between your Internet Service Provider (ISP) and your local network.

---

# What I Learned

Understanding the purpose of each network device helps explain how modern networks communicate securely and efficiently.
